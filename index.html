Cases

AsureQuality

Built a computer vision system that inspects meat carcasses on the processing line, flagging defects in real time so operators can trim and process each product accurately. Precise defect detection is critical for export compliance—missed or mislabelled defects cause shipments to be turned away at the importing country's border. The system supports faster throughput while reducing rejection risk and quality-related losses.

KPMG

Designing and deploying AI agents that automate repetitive internal workflows—from document handling and data lookups to routine reporting. The agents integrate directly with existing systems, freeing consultants and analysts to focus on higher-value client work and materially shortening turnaround on operational tasks.

Gyre Energy

Advised on the end-to-end system architecture for moving telemetry from Gyre's connected fridge units out to the edge. The recommended design optimises for bandwidth, latency, and operating cost—enabling reliable real-time monitoring across a distributed hardware estate without over-provisioning cloud infrastructure.

EU Commission

Designed and owned the entity resolution algorithm that matches sanctioned individuals and organisations against applicants for European Commission grant funding. The system underpinned oversight of approximately €2 trillion in monitored grant allocations, giving investigators a scalable, accurate way to detect risk exposure at the point of application.
